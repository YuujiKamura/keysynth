Dataset summary:
  - 2100 multi-track MIDI compositions rendered through high-quality
    sample-based synthesis
  - Per-instrument stems (drums, bass, guitar, piano, strings, etc.)
  - Total size ~145 GB — too large to mirror in this repo

Use in this corpus:
  Slakh2100 distributes a `metadata.yaml` per track listing instrument
  classes. We index the metadata only and select up to 5 tracks whose
  guitar stem is the dominant solo voice for inclusion under
  bench-out/REF/guitar/corpus/popular/. Only the guitar stem WAV +
  the corresponding MIDI track from the multi-track .mid (extracted
  to a single-track guitar MIDI via `mido`) are referenced.

  WAV files are NOT redistributed. The `fetch.sh --slakh-sample N`
  mode downloads the Nth selected guitar stem on demand; without
  the flag only metadata is materialised. The MIDI ground truth and
  the manifest.json entries (track id, channel, source URL, attribution
  string) ARE committed.
