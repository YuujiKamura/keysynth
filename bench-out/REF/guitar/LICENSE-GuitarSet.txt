Dataset summary:
  - 360 audio clips × 30 seconds, ~5 GB total
  - Multiple microphones (hex pickup + reference mic) per clip
  - Aligned MIDI ground truth + chord labels
  - Six players, varied styles (rock / singer-songwriter / jazz / bossa
    nova / funk)

Use in this corpus:
  Single-note samples (each open string + selected fretted positions)
  are extracted from GuitarSet's solo passages and normalised to
  44.1 kHz / mono / 16-bit PCM under bench-out/REF/guitar/single_note/.
  Strummed-chord samples are extracted from comping passages under
  bench-out/REF/guitar/chord/. The MIDI files are pulled directly
  from the per-clip jam annotations.

  WAV files are NOT redistributed in this repository. They are
  fetched on demand by `bench-out/REF/guitar/fetch.sh`. The MIDI
  ground truth and the manifest.json provenance entries IS committed
  alongside this LICENSE file so the audit trail is preserved.
